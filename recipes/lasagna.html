Lasagna

Our delicious lasagna

This lasagna combines mozzarella, mince, and a creamy sauce

Ingredients

  • Mince meat
  • mozzarella
  • cream sauce

Steps

  1. Make a simple meat sauce with some toms and mince meat
  2. Layer in a dish the cream sauce and the meat sauce
  3. Top with a generous helping of mozzarella
  4. Bake for an hour or so
