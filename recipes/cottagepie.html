Cottage Pie

Our delicious Cottage Pie

This Cottage Pie combines CARROTS, Lamb mince, and mash potato

Ingredients

  • Lamb mince meat
  • CARROTS
  • Mash Potato

Steps

  1. Make a simple meat sauce with some toms and lamb meat
  2. Mix in the CARROTS
  3. Layer in a dish the meat sauce
  4. Top with a generous helping of mash potato
  5. Bake for an hour or so
