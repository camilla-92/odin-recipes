Shepherds Pie

Our delicious Shepherds Pie

This Shepherds combines PEAS, mince, and a mash potato

Ingredients

  • Mince meat
  • PEAS
  • Mash Potato

Steps

  1. Make a simple meat sauce with some toms and mince meat
  2. Mix in the
    PEAS
  3. Layer in a dish the meat sauce
  4. Top with a generous helping of mash potato
  5. Bake for an hour or so
